Ingredients:

Crumble
 - 225 g / 8 oz plain flour
 - 115 g / 4 oz butter
 - 90 g / 3 oz sugar
 - pinch of salt

Fruit
 - 455 g / 1 lb fruit, washed and prepared
 - 3 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven at 200 degrees C / 400 degrees F / gas 6.

 2. Mix the crumble ingredients by rubbing the mixture between your hands until it resembles fine breadcrumbs.
 
 3. Put the fruit in a shallow ovenproof dish and sprinkle with the sugar. Spread the crumble mix over the fruit.
 
 4. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden.
